Rose Garden In The City of Roses

在波特兰风景秀丽的华盛顿公园里，隐藏着一座国际玫瑰实验园。它是波特兰市一颗璀璨的明珠， 城市也因之获得“玫瑰之都”的美誉。玫瑰园以其秀美、芬芳以及悠久的历史吸引着来自世界各地的参观者。

玫瑰园创建于第一次世界大战期间的1917年，原本作为保护欧洲杂交玫瑰免遭战火毁灭的庇护所。 如今它已绽放成一座生机盎然的玫瑰博物馆，收藏着近650个不同品种、超过一万株玫瑰植株。它既是新品种的试验场， 也是获奖品种的展示台，让游客感受多姿多彩的玫瑰种植技术与创新。

玫瑰园独具匠心地利用山谷地形设计成阶梯状。站在园中，俯瞰繁华的城市中心，远眺巍峨的胡德雪山， 环顾秀丽的四周风景，让人心旷神怡。初夏的上午，太阳穿过白云，洒在公园里，花儿显得格外的鲜艳。 络绎不绝的游客徜徉于蜿蜒的观花步道，园中不时传来阵阵悠扬的琴声。在一个鲜花拥簇的角落里，一位温文尔雅的老太太悠闲地坐在摇椅上， 旁若无人地读书。这里每一种花都详细地标注了品名和特性，不管是茶香玫花瓣的精致还是杂交品种天鹅绒般的丝滑， 玫瑰园的每一个角落都能给人一个视觉惊喜。

除了展示精湛的园艺，玫瑰园更是让人轻松、给人启迪。它让游人放慢脚步，吸一口玫瑰的馨香， 在喧嚣的城市中与大自然进行一次交流。在不同季节，这里还举办的各种活动，包括科普教育，专业导游等，进一步丰富游客的游览体验。

作为波特兰的城市标志，玫瑰园体现了该市致力于追求自然美与自豪感的精神。参观玫瑰园不只是走马观花， 更是对这座玫瑰之城精神的一种沉浸式体验。

Nestled in the scenic hills of Washington Park, the International Rose Test Garden stands as one of Portland, Oregon’s most treasured landmarks. Known proudly as the “City of Roses,” Portland celebrates its floral heritage through this remarkable garden, which draws visitors from around the world with its beauty, fragrance, and history.

Established in 1917, the garden was originally created as a safe haven for hybrid rose varieties during World War I. Today, it has blossomed into a vibrant living museum, home to more than 10,000 rose bushes representing nearly 650 distinct varieties. The garden serves as both a testing ground for new roses and a showcase of award-winning blooms, allowing visitors to experience the diversity and innovation of rose cultivation.

The garden’s thoughtfully designed terraces offer breathtaking views of downtown Portland, Mount Hood, and the surrounding landscape. On an early summer morning, the sun filtered through the white clouds and spilled into the park, making the flowers appear especially vibrant. A steady stream of visitors wandered along the winding flower-viewing path, accompanied now and then by the sound of melodious music. In a corner surrounded by blossoms, a refined old lady sat leisurely in a rocking chair, reading her book as though oblivious to the world around her. Each variety of plant here is labeled to highlight its unique characteristics. Whether it is the delicate petals of a tea rose or the rich, velvety tones of a hybrid variety, every corner of the garden offers a new visual delight.

Beyond its horticultural significance, the International Rose Test Garden is a place of relaxation and inspiration. It invites guests to slow down, breathe in the sweet aroma of roses, and connect with nature in an urban setting. Seasonal events, educational programs, and guided tours further enrich the visitor experience.

As a symbol of Portland’s identity, the Rose Garden embodies the city’s commitment to natural beauty and community pride. A visit to this floral sanctuary is not just a stroll among flowers—it is an immersion into the spirit of the City of Roses itself.
